With the [[Advertise with NeoLink!|NeoLink]] project appearing to no longer be maintained or updated Tabby created TabbyAds as a replacement.

Similar to NeoLink, TabbyAds is a small banner network for sites hosted with [Neocities](https://neocities.org/).
